About Us

Lan Xin Notes - Your Operational Growth Partner

Our Story

Lan Xin Notes was founded in 2022 as a platform dedicated to sharing operational knowledge. Our founder, Lan Xin, has over 10 years of operational experience and has worked at several well-known internet companies, responsible for dozens of cross-border e-commerce and content creation projects.

Our team members come from different industry backgrounds, including cross-border e-commerce experts, content creation mentors, AI tool researchers, and video marketing consultants, all committed to providing practical and cutting-edge operational knowledge to operators and entrepreneurs.

Our goal is to help every learner improve their operational capabilities, solve practical work problems, and achieve personal and business growth through professional operational notes, practical case analyses, and cutting-edge tool introductions.
