Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled the most common questions and answers to help you quickly find the information you need

How do I become a member?

+

We currently offer a single membership service: Knowledge Planet annual membership for $218, providing year-round professional content and services.

Through the Knowledge Planet platform, members can access all professional content, participate in online discussions, receive one-on-one consultations, and more.

What are the membership benefits?

+

Lan Xin Notes members can enjoy the following benefits:

  • Access to all premium content and tutorials
  • Participate in weekly online sharing sessions
  • Get exclusive operational tools and resources
  • Join exclusive community to network with other operational experts
  • Opportunity to participate in offline salon events
  • Content customization services

Can I get a refund for my membership?

+

You can apply for a full refund within 3 days of purchasing membership. Refunds require contacting our customer service team via email (support@lanxinnotes.com) and explaining the reason for the refund.

After 3 days, refunds will not be available. Thank you for your understanding.

How do I access course content?

+

After becoming a member, you can directly access all course content in the "Course Center" on the website. Each course includes videos, written materials, and practical assignments. You can learn at your own pace anytime.

How often is content updated?

+

We update at least 3 high-quality articles and 1 professional video tutorial per week. Additionally, we launch 1-2 complete courses monthly, covering AI tools, cross-border e-commerce, content creation, and membership operations.

Can I download course content?

+

Most course PDF materials and tool templates support downloads, but video content is only available for online viewing and not for download to protect intellectual property.

How do I get an invoice?

+

After successful payment, you can apply for an invoice in the "My Orders" page in the "Member Center". We support electronic invoices, which are generally sent to your registered email within 3 business days.

What if I don't receive a confirmation email after payment?

+

After successful payment, the system will automatically send a confirmation email to your registered email. If you haven't received it, please check the following:

  • Check your spam folder
  • Confirm your registered email is correct
  • Wait 5-10 minutes, emails may be delayed

If you still haven't received it, please contact customer service (support@lanxinnotes.com) for assistance.

What if I forget my password?

+

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and we'll send a password reset link to your email. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

Why can't I access certain content?

+

If you can't access certain content, possible reasons include:

  • Content is for members only, please check your membership status
  • Your membership has expired, please renew
  • Browser cache issues, try clearing cache or using a different browser
  • Network connection issues, check your internet connection

If the problem persists, please contact technical support.

How do I contact customer service?

+

You can contact us through the following methods:

  • Email: support@lanxinnotes.com
  • Online customer service: Available on the website from 9 AM to 6 PM
  • WeChat: Add our official WeChat account for consultation

We typically respond within 24 hours and will do our best to resolve your issues.
